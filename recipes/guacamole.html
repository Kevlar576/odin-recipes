Guacamole

Kevlars Guacamole

Ingredients

  1. 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  2. 1 teaspoon salt
  3. 1 lime, juiced
  4. ½ cup diced onion
  5. 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  6. 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  7. 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  8. 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Directions

  1. Step 1 In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
